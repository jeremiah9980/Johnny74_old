Character Profile

His Journey

The Making of #74

2023 · Foundation

Built in Georgetown, Starting Young

Johnny grew up in Georgetown, TX, joining GYBA's Romo's Arsenal and contributing to a season that ended with three championships — opening tournament, regular season title, end-of-season crown. Learning what winning looks like early sets a standard you don't let go of.

2025 · Bananas Season

Contact, Speed, and Execution

The 2025 Bananas season is documented clip by clip in the film room above. A player who understands his role: get on base, be a threat, do the small things that move runners. Singles, walks, stolen bases — and when the moment is right, the power to go extra bases. The home run was one swing. The consistency is the real story.

Fall 2025 · Growth

Expanding the Game

By fall 2025, extra-base range emerged. The sacrifice bunts show IQ: willingness to give himself up when the situation calls for it. That's not something you can coach. That's character built into how he plays the game.
